Treasure Hunters Hunter Championship Introduction



Rules: 4 vs 4 elimination battle with no respawn.
Hunter Competition has 2 different maps: a round-shaped map and a more ring-shaped map, which will be chosen randomly once matchmaking is completed. Next, we will analyze the key characteristics of these two maps:
Round Space

The round map is more narrow and teams will spawn in on 2 elevated platforms. There is not much space to dodge or hide from attacks and you will be forced to face the enemy team no matter which direction you head. Due to lack of space, teams with high burst damage tend to succeed on this map.
Formation: it is recommended that the team have at least one tank to avoid being completed shredded by enemy DPS characters. In addition to the tank, 2 or 3 DPS characters are recommended in order to put pressure on the enemy. A healer is good to help keep enemy DPS from getting out of control, but it is up to the team on whether or not a healer would be essential. The narrow nature of the map can cause teams to clump together, making the team susceptible to enemy control skills, so be sure to keep that in mind when choosing characters and navigating the map.
Recommended team: (control team) Drake + Charlie + Mary + Silver Seeker OR (burst team) Drake + Mary x2 + Silver Seeker
Ring Space

The ring-shaped map is symmetrical, with one team spawning on point A and the other spawning on point B. The map leads in a circle, so skilled ranged hunters can easily kite melee damage. Assassin type characters will prove more effective in getting at the enemy's backline. Formation: due to the shape of the map, a kiting team with Charlie as the core can use Charlie's ice abilities to control and slow the enemy, dragging out their formation and allowing you to burst down their characters. Another strategy is to charge the enemy head-on, while letting Harry or Charlie charge the enemy from the other side, creating confusion and ruining the enemy's formation.
Recommended team: Drake + Mary + Silver Seeker + Harry, OR Charlie x2 + Mary + Silver Seeker
