Treasure Raiders Game Background



This is one of the most exciting times human history has ever known. With the awakening of Ancient Civilization Power, over the course of one night, this old planet we live on produced countless treasures with mysterious powers. Humanity ushered in an era of chasing after dreams, expeditions, and precious treasures. However, only true adventurers were able to get a glance of this other world; they proudly called themselves "Treasure Raiders."

Searching after treasure also comes with great risks, and ancient treasures contain dangerous powers that could threaten the peace of this world. In order to provide support to the Treasure Raiders, and to protect and safely acquire more treasure, the Treasure Raiders Society was founded. The Society began to register and manage all the Treasure Raiders and began to evaluate who were the strongest hunters, and in turn began to help to dispatch hunters
